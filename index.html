Meet Darren Murphy, a COPD patient who used Botanical Farms CBD Gummies

For 33 years, Darren Murphy smoked nearly a pack per day. In the back of his mind he knew it would catch up to him eventually, but never seemed to kick the habit. It wasn't until tragedy struck that he decided to quit smoking for good. Three years ago Darren's wife died of lung cancer, leaving him with 2 kids and 6 grandchildren. After mourning the loss of his wife, Darren decided to quit for good.

"After losing Lisa to lung cancer, I knew it was just a matter of time until this bad habit caught up to me and would leave my grandkids without grandparents to watch them grow up. I just couldn't live with that thought," said Murphy

In 2018 Darren quit smoking cold turkey after his wife's passing. After he finally quit, he thought all of his worries were over...the years following, the condition of his lungs continued to deteriorate. In 2020, his doctor diagnosed him with Stage 3 COPD.

"I thought that quitting was all I had to do to protect my future...I was terribly wrong. After quitting, my COPD symptoms got worse and worse. The shortness of breath, constant coughing, and lungs filled with phlegm made daily living miserable."

As if Stage 3 COPD, and losing his wife to lung cancer wasn't enough... Darren contracted COVID-19 in the middle of the pandemic. He was put on a ventilator and spent two weeks in the hospital fighting for his life. Due to the state of his lungs, doctors weren't optimistic about his recovery.

Miraculously, Darren was able to fight off COVID-19 and make a full recovery. His lungs however, were still struggling with Stage-3 COPD. His doctors recommended a few treatments but none of them were able to clear any of his symptoms; he was utterly hopeless
